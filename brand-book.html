02 — Misuse

Don't Do This

Keep the mark honest. Protect proportions, color, and orientation so it stays instantly recognizable.

Stretch

Never distort proportions.

Recolor

Don't swap the brand orange.

Rotate

Keep it level on the road.

Busy BG

Use clean, high-contrast fields.

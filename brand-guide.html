The Face Behind the Shop

Twenty-two years as a neighborhood shop isn't a slogan — it's a person. We put a face to the name so customers know exactly who's standing behind the work.

"AT All Model Repair, you're a name we know with a car we've probably seen before."

Jano has run All Model Repair for over two decades, building trust one honest estimate at a time. When the brand speaks, it should sound like him: straight, warm, and sure of the work — the mechanic a neighbor sends a neighbor to.

05

Tone of Voice

We sound like the most honest mechanic you've ever met — because that's the reputation we've earned. Plain-spoken, warm, and sure of the work.

Honest

Tell people the truth and the price up front. No upsell, no jargon, no surprises.

Neighborly

Warm and local. We know customers by name and remember their cars.

Confident

Decades of every make and model. We've seen it, and we can fix it.

Plain-Spoken

Short, clear sentences a driver actually understands. Skip the shop-talk.

Say"Here's what it needs and what it'll cost — your call."
Not"Per our diagnostic assessment, remediation is advised."
Say"We work on every make and model."
Not"Servicing a curated selection of vehicle marques."

From the Bay

Service Stickers
